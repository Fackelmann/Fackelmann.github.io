Ducky One 2 Mini

Daniel Gonzalez Plaza

July 26, 2019

Filed under “Stuff”

For years I have been thinking about getting a mechanical keyboard. When I tried them I loved the feeling of the keys and the noise.

Recently I found the community /r/mechanicalkeyboards in reddit and I learnt about 60% keyboards. I loved the idea of having a compact keyboard the very moment I saw them.

After much searching I decided that a custom build keyboard was an overkill for my first keyboard (although I’m already following some massdrops), and settled for the Ducky One 2 Mini.

This keyboard costs less than $100 ($99 to be precise). As I said it’s a 60% keyboard, which means […]. It also has PBS keycaps. This type of plastic doesn’t get shinny with time.

For the switches I chose Cherry MX Blue. These kind of switches have an awesome noise and feeling when pressed. Since the main place I will use it will be at home, I didn’t mind (in fact, I liked) it being very loud.
